About UNIVERSITY MANAGEMENT PORTAL


University management system is built in a student-faculty friendly environment where a faculty can update marks to students of whatever subject he takes of all the components, and it generates the grade of the subject. Also a student can see his marks updated by faculty of all components and subject grade. This marks management system is developed using python , flask and sqllite is the database used. With this system you can easily manage marks for university students. The project has a provision where lecturers can upload student marks and the marks and grades get added directly to the portal.
